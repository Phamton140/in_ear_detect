in ear detect is a musical app that lets you identify notes and keys in real time using your device's microphone.

Main features:
• Real-time musical note detection.
• Chromatic indicator with detected notes.
• Suggested most likely keys.
• Recent note history.
• Dark, modern, and easy-to-use interface.

Perfect for musicians, students, or anyone who wants to practice ear training. The app processes audio locally and does not send data to external servers.

Developer: Melquisedec Sarfeliz.
