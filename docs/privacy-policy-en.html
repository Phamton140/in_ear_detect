Privacy Policy for in ear detect

Last updated: June 25, 2026

Developer: Melquisedec Sarfeliz

Contact email: genesismsf@gmail.com

1. General Information

in ear detect is a musical application that detects notes and keys in real time using the device microphone. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what your rights are.

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. The application does not require user registration and does not ask for your name, email, location, or any other identifiable information.

The only permission the app requests is access to the microphone, which is necessary to analyze audio and display detected notes.

3. Microphone Usage

Audio captured by the microphone is processed locally on your device only. It is not recorded, stored, or transmitted to any external server. Analysis happens in real time and audio data is discarded immediately after processing.

4. Third-Party Services

in ear detect does not use analytics, advertising, or third-party SDKs that collect information. We do not share data with third parties.

5. Data Security

Since we do not store or transmit user data, there is no risk of personal information leakage through our application.

6. User Rights

You can revoke the microphone permission at any time from your Android device settings. Doing so will prevent note detection, but no personal data will be deleted because none is stored.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with the corresponding update date.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us at genesismsf@gmail.com.
